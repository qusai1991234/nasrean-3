About the Clinic

A space where healing meets expertise.

"At our clinic, healing happens naturally — mind, body, and soul."

Dr. Nesrin Al-Dabbas is a highly esteemed consultant psychiatrist based in Amman, Jordan. She earned her medical degree from the Jordan University of Science and Technology and completed her residency at King Abdullah University Hospital, also training as Chief Resident in Al-Rashid Hospital Centre.

Dr. Al-Dabbas achieved first place in the Jordanian Board Certificate in Psychiatry, and obtained a Master's degree in Psychiatry with distinction from Cardiff University, UK — ranking first among her peers.

  • MD, Jordan University of Science and Technology
  • JBPsych — Jordanian Board Certificate in Psychiatry (1st place)
  • MSc Psychiatry with distinction, Cardiff University UK (1st place)
  • Founder & Director, Dr. Nesrin Al-Dabbas Psychiatry Clinic
